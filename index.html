Spinny

Show things during form submit or AJAX calls.

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Spinny makes it easy to show a progress indicator during form submit or AJAX calls.

For instance, you may want to show a progress indicator while a form is submitting. You may want to show a similar indicator when an AJAX request in progress, and then show a message upon success or failure.

Known issues

  • Form submit indicator does not show on Safari. The form submits just fine, though.
